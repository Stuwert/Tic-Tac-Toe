Tic Tac Toe

How To Play the Game

This is your basic game of Tic Tac Toe. The notification of the screen lets you know which player's turn it is.

The objective is to make a line of your pieces (either X or Y), such that there are three of them in a row.

This may be done diagonally, in rows, or in columns.

At the end of each game, you may choose to restart.
